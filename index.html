About Me

I’m Waquar Walid, and I come from Katihar, Bihar. Currently, I’m pursuing my Master’s in Business Administration with a focus on Human Resources at Dr. Vishwanath Karad MIT-World Peace University in Pune. I completed my Bachelor’s in Business Administration from Galgotias University, Greater Noida. Over the years, I’ve developed a passion for HR, with a particular interest in talent acquisition, HR analytics, and communication. During my journey, I’ve had the opportunity to work on various aspects of HR, including recruitment planning, candidate screening, conducting interviews, and supporting onboarding processes. These experiences have taught me the importance of building strong, collaborative relationships and using data to drive HR decisions that truly benefit both individuals and organizations. I’m always eager to learn and grow, and I’m excited to keep applying what I’ve learned in the real world. My goal is to contribute meaningfully to the HR field, making workplaces more efficient, transparent, and inclusive.

Blog

“UNITY IN DIVERSITY”

Diversity and inclusion are two words we hear often in today’s world, especially in the workplace. However, these concepts go far beyond just professional settings, growing up as a Muslim, I’ve often faced discrimination, both socially and at work. These experiences have shown me how important diversity and inclusion really are, not just in the workplace but in all aspects of life. I’ve learned how crucial it is to create spaces where people are celebrated for who they are, not judged by their background or identity. Having personally experienced these challenges, I understand deeply how diversity and inclusion are essential for creating environments where everyone feels valued, respected, and able to thrive.
